SAFARI DESTINATION

East Africa is a wild kingdom of extraordinary cultures, people, landscapes, and wildlife mingled in the world's most spectacular side of the world continent.

Most popular destinations in East Africa in 2023/24:

  1. Serengeti National Park - Tanzania
  2. Masai Mara - Kenya
  3. Bwindi Impenetrable Forest - Uganda
  4. Volcanoes National Park - Rwanda
  5. Ngorongoro Crater - Kenya
  6. Zanzibar - Tanzania
  7. Kibale National Park - Uganda
  8. Kidepo Valley National Park - Uganda

Come have a feeling of driving to the wilderness and have the best memories in life.

As your journey goes on, expect a variety of wild animals and beautiful scenery.
